Warehouse Inventory Rack Management

The Inventory program manages the placement of bulk goods by the appropriate appropriate empty shelf that fits the height and weight of the pallet. The recording of the bulk goods rack and shelf location is placed with in a table.
When the program is executed for the first time, it will prompt the user to define the location of racks and shelves, detail their height and weight limitations.
Further detials can be added, such as Racks that reside in secure locations, refrigeration, or shelves for dangerous goods.
