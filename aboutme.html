About me

A Melbournian since the age of 1

My parents migrated from Macedonia to Australia in 1966. Taking on the Melbournian culture I grew up watching football, cricket and tennis eating meat pies and fish and chips.

After a long career in IT administration, I have taken time off to complete a software development course that includes Ruby, Rails, HTML, CSS, Javascripts, and React at the Coder Factory Acedemy in order to become an accomplished full stack developer.

I have a background in database management as well as Backup and Storage administration which has provided me a strong base in becoming a great coder. I developed a knack for coding when I had a need to code scripts in Rexx, Korn, and Bash to automate manual processes while in I.T. administrations.

In my late teens I started as a Tape and Print jockey within a computer department ensuring mount requests for data on tape were processed efficiently and prints dispatched on time. Soon afterwards I was promoted as an Operations Analyst where I provisioned resources to ensure a successful overnight batch schedule. My interests lead me to database administration, where I was more involed in projects, organizing table structures, and tuning SQL code.

Opportunities took me Backup and Storage management, dealing with data backup and recovery strategies, provisoning storage, volume replication, disk encryption, and disaster recovery planning.

Outside of IT I love the AFL sport where I get to go to games with friends and enjoy the pace of the match especially when Geelong wins. I am also an avid foodie fan, I particularly enjoy Thai cuisines and street festivals, but most of love the chase of finding the next great eat.
